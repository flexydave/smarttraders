Some Of The Things You May Want To Know

We answer questions so you don't have to ask them

How can I get started selling gift cards?

To start selling, you have to create an account on a platform like ours.
Sign up with these easy steps:

  • Register with your email and phone number
  • Verify same mail with the OTP sent to it.
  • Set your password and PIN (we advise you on a strong password you will remember), and you can start selling.

How are rates determined?

Rates are updated frequently, multiple times daily, as the market value is volatile. The rate you would get for your card depends on two things; The type and amount of gift card. Using the FlipEX rate calculator will help you quickly determine what your gift card is worth.

Why have I not received payment?

This could be due to the network of the receiving bank. In such situations, we advise you to try again in a few minutes and if it persists, you can reach out to us at support@smartraders.com.

I did not make the transaction, what do I do?

If you notice a trade was made on your account and it wasn't you, change your account password and reach out swiftly to support@smartraders.com.

Is there a limit to the transactions I can make?

There is no limit to the transactions you can make on your account.
